How To Play

  • Start typing. The letters will appear in the boxes
  • Remove letters with Backspace
  • Hit Enter/Return to submit an answer
  • Letters with green backgrounds are in the right spot
  • Letters with yellow backgrounds exist in the word, but aren't in the right spot
  • Letters with gray backgrounds don't exist in the word
  • If you need a hint, click the ? icon
